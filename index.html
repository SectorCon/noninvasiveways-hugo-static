NonInvasive Ways

The future of universally applicable physiological monitoring technology can only be non-invasive. It has to be riskless, painless as well as cost-efficient. And it has to be made accessible across the globe.

This is our conviction.

Advanced sensor technology, microelectronics and digitalization offer us the means to crucially improve non-invasive monitoring procedures. We have to use this potential to improve health care for everyone.

For this reason our initiative offers transparency and security through an open source ecosystem. It incorporates separate modules for research and development, financing and digital business models using open data formats and open interfaces at low costs.

If we simplyfy diagnostic methods, while also
making them safer, we can improve medical care.
This is what we want to achieve.
Step by step.

Open Source Ecosystem

Our initiative offers transparency and security through an open source ecosystem. Based on a meritocratic system we will develop and provide open and manufacturer independent interfaces, algorithms, software modules and data access.

Our Mission

The future of universally applicable physiological monitoring technology can only be non-invasive. It has to be riskless, painless as well as cost-efficient. And it has to be made available across the globe.

Research & Development

In order to advance non-invasive monitoring technology and clinical research we offer a catalog of software modules and algorithms. The catalog covers data synchronisation, filtering and analysis and will be continually expanded.

Financing

To close the gap between reasearch funding and market entry, we offer a cooperative financing model, which is especially suited for small and medium enterprises and serves as an alternative to classical venture capital funding.

Community

In order to improve the status quo of physiological monitoring, we will have to overcome enormous challenges. We are convinced that this can be best achieved in a joint effort of all interested parties.

Regulatory Approval

Our goal is to support our members in the regulatory approval process by offering modular approval documentation. The catalog will adress risk managment, software verification and validation as well as usability.
